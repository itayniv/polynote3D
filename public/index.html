About the project

Polynote is a multiplayer sequencer in the browser!


1. Each 'poly' represents a different sound

2. Click / Tap on the grid to generate different patterns

3. A short press will generate a small and quite polynote

4. A long press will generate a big and loud polynote

5. To delete a poly just click / tap on it again


Try it out, make some noise.
